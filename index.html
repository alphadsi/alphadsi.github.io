About ΔΣI

History

Delta Sigma Iota was founded in August of 2000 in order to promote unity, spread truth, value education and integrate South Asian culture into our daily lives.
We are a Fraternity that stresses the importance of culture, unity and academics and continually works to provide the South Asian community at Penn State as well as elsewhere with assistance in any way possible.

Founding Fathers

Five unique men with five unique strengths combined in order to form the Delta Sigma Iota Fraternity. Vishal Desai, Vipul Patel, Roby Palakudy, Tarak Bhavsar and Rupesh Patel along with Chapter Advisor Benny Joseph, joined forces in order to create the first South-Asian based, Social Service Fraternity at Penn State University.

Our Mission:

Delta Sigma Iota is built on three strong pillars: SERVICE, UNITY, and EDUCATION.
In 2000, the foundation was laid down by five friends, who saw these visions, and made it a reality for many. As a Fraternity, we demonstrate the importance of culture, unity, and academics through our actions and continuous participation in actively serving surrounding communities.
